Research & Software Projects

1. Schenker-styled Music Resynthesis from Liszt Transcendental Etude No.4 Mazeppa and La Campanella (2021)(Ongoing)

Mazeppa and La Campanella are two of the most complicated and intriguing piano solo music for virtuoso musicians in musical performance history. This project aims to demystify the complex-sounding of both pieces by separating melodic embellishment and rhythmic add-ons from the harmony planning and other structural textures. After breaking down the music into the prime structures, I will try to entangle the prime structure from Mazeppa to La Campanella to create a new musical piece aligned with Listz’s aesthetics.

Though previous researchers have used traditional DSP methods, such as using chroma features, with an Artificial Neural Network to classify the chord in the given corpus and obtain effective results, the granularity of the outputs and the musicality inferred from the outputs are limited. This study proposed a new paradigm for the ACR task, which uses massive artificial datasets synthesized under the domain rules of multiple genres, including Asian-pop, Euro-pop, EDM, and classical music, to improve the classification result of the models. The model’s outputs include midi transcription of the chord and the instrumentation information with different levels of granularity.

3. 减字和声谱 (Jianzi harmony symbol system): Extended Harmony Symbols: an advanced roman numeral with voice leading and inversion information(2021)

Musical chord symbols are widely known as a compressed notation of the vertical structures of given segmentation of music. The roman numeral system conveys more tonal relationship information than the jazz chords system relying on absolute pitch representation. However, roman numerals can be designed more informed by adding relative-melody-position and secondary analysis information (T, PD, D). Being inspired by Jianzi Pu(减字谱)–a tablature notation for a Chinese zither called Guqin, this symbolic system attempts to describe the harmonic structures for a musical chord. This symbolic system not only conveys information about chord quality(Maj, min, etc.), base-root relationship (inversion), and scale degree (I, II, or vi), but it also informs the melody-root relationship, functional analysis, and secondary tonicization information in a reader-friendly design for Asian musicians.

4. Harmony2Vec: A Music Theory endorsed Chord
Embedding for Machine Learning (2021)

Harmony2Vec is a musicianship-enhanced embedding for chord sequence learning in machine learning. Previously, scholars used pitch set representation to extract chord information from a series of midi messages. However, a more musical knowledge-informed method is to convert the pitch set tuple to roman numerals, melody-and-bass-information, and chord functions in the context of being tonal. This extra layer of musical abstraction from midi may boost the musical performances learned from midi corpses.

Music phylogenetics – A survey on computational approach for music evolution across time and genre(2021)(Ongoing)

In biology, phylogenetics is a term to describe the evolutionary relationship between organisms and In biology, phylogenetics is a term to describe the evolutionary relationship between organisms and species based on the phenotype and genotype similarity analysis. Phylogenetics is also studied as a linguistics concept showing the evolution connection between different language phyla. By drawing an analogy between music and languages, which share many similarities in the mode of creation, transmission, and cultural consumption, music pieces can also be treated as an organism that evolved from their musical parents. 6. Constant Q Transform-based chord detection on F0-frequency exclusive timbre (2019)

This project transcribes chord information from a sinewave-timbre exclusive audio file. It mainly applies Constant Q Transform with amplitude threshold to extract the sinewave component in given wave files. This algorithm can also be applied to harmonics extraction software with music composed by different instruments’ timbres.

7. Bsynth – a VST3 additive synth plugin(2019)

Bsynth is a VST3 additive synthesizer plugin written in C++ with the JUCE library. It has the synthesizer’s basic functionality, including a selection of a basic oscillator waveform, a low pass and high pass filter, and an ADSR envelope.

8. 蝶(Butterfly) – A text2chord pentatonic piano music generation system based on Hashing Algorithm(2019)

Butterfly is a software generating pentatonic music based on user input sentences. It uses supercollider as front-end and Common Music as its back-end. This software utilizes Open Sound Control and Hash Table to match the ASCII representation of English characters to a predefined chord in terms of its roman numerals meaning. Humanization techniques are also applied to the rendered audio output.

Hardware Projects

1. Haptic Music Suit- Music Sensation Augmentation via wearable vibrator matrix(2021)(ongoing)

This research project adds a track of haptic motors on the human body to augment the musical experience. Music is widely known as the art of vibration, though most of the time, people are talking about the vibration of the air, but why can’t it be a vibration that triggers somatosensory pleasure in human brains? For example, when musicians make a Melodic Dubstep beat, they can assign an extra track in Digital Audio Workstation to create a mode of vibration actuated by the vibrators matrix attached to human bodies. A musician can decide the haptic patterns and dynamics of the motor matrix on the body correlating with the auditory experience of music.

2. Midi4all – a MIDI performance system with force-sensitive QWERTY keyboard Design(2021)(ongoing)

This project integrates the MIDI performance system into a QWERTY keyboard. Nearly everybody in the world enjoys the pleasure brought by listening to music. However, most people who want to learn to play a musical instrument are hindered by their financial situation. Why not convert these keyboarders of text into keyboarders of music? Therefore, I am designing a qwerty music paradigm that enables everybody to play and improvise musical pieces just by QWERTY keyboard and a new music theory learning pedagogy designed for the QWERTY musicians.

3. Musical Brain-Computer Interface – An experiment for a new paradigm of chord learning pedagogy(2021)(Ongoing)

Brain-computer-interface is a subfield of Human-Computer-Interface that uses electrophysiological brain signals to trigger specific computer actions. In musical pedagogy, the learning process of musical chords can only be achieved by keyboard and lute-family instruments. And there are no other instrument choices for a musical beginner to learn the polyphonic structure of music. Therefore, a BCI device that captures users’ mental commands can help beginner musicians play out musical chords immediately without knowing how to play other musical instruments such as piano and guitar. This work is temporarily implemented in the Emotiv Epoc-X headset.
